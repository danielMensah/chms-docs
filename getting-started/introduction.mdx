Bethel ChMS is a church management system built for churches of every size, from a single congregation to a multi-branch network. It gives pastors, office staff, and branch administrators one place to manage the people and activities that keep your church running.

{/* Screenshot: Bethel ChMS dashboard overview */}

## What you can do with Bethel ChMS

- **Manage members:** Store contact details, group members by branch, and keep your directory up to date.
- **Track attendance:** Record who attended each service or meeting and spot trends over time.
- **Run events:** Create services, meetings, and special events so your team knows what's coming.
- **Handle visitors:** Log first-time guests and follow up so no one slips through the cracks.

<Note>
**What you'll need to get started:** an email address and a few minutes. That's it.
</Note>

## Key terms

Before you dive in, here are a few terms you'll see throughout Bethel ChMS:

- **Organisation:** Your church network. It contains all your branches, members, and settings.
- **Branch:** An individual church location within your organisation.
- **Role:** The level of access a person has in Bethel ChMS (for example, Branch Admin or Editor).
