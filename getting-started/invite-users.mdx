Now that your church is set up, invite the people who'll help manage it. Each person you invite gets a specific role that controls what they can see and do in Bethel ChMS.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open user management">
    In the sidebar, click **Users**. This opens the user management page where you can see everyone who has access to your organisation and send new invitations.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send an invitation">
    Click **Invite user** and fill in the form:

    | Field      | Required | Notes |
    | ---------- | -------- | ----- |
    | **Email**  | Yes      | The email address where the invitation will be sent. |
    | **Name**   | Yes      | The person's display name in Bethel ChMS. |
    | **Role**   | Yes      | Choose from Organisation Admin, Branch Admin, or Editor. |
    | **Branch** | Sometimes | Required when you select a branch-scoped role (Branch Admin or Editor). |

    {/* Screenshot: invite user form with role dropdown */}

    Click **Send invitation**. Bethel ChMS sends the invitee an email with a magic link they can use to accept.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose the right role">
    Not sure which role to pick? Here's a quick summary:

    - **Organisation Admin:** Full access across every branch. Good for senior pastors or headquarters staff who oversee the whole network.
    - **Branch Admin:** Manages members, attendance, and events for a specific branch. Ideal for local pastors or office managers.
    - **Editor:** Can add and update records within a branch but cannot change settings or invite others.

    For a full breakdown of what each role can do, see [Roles and permissions](/guides/roles-and-permissions).
  </Step>

  <Step title="What the invitee sees">
    The invitee receives an email with a magic link. When they click it, they land on the **Accept invitation** page, which shows:

    - The name of your organisation
    - The role they've been assigned
    - The branch they belong to (if applicable)

    They set a password (minimum 8 characters) and they're in. No extra setup required on their end.

    {/* Screenshot: invitation email / accept invitation page */}
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
**Who can invite whom:**
- **Organisation Owner** can invite any role.
- **Organisation Admin** can invite Organisation Admins, Branch Admins, and Editors.
- **Branch Admin** can invite Branch Admins and Editors within their own branch.
- **Editor** cannot invite anyone.
</Info>

<Note>
Invitations expire after 7 days. If someone misses theirs, go back to the **Users** page and resend it.
</Note>
